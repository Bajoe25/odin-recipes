Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian baked pasta dish with rich meat sauce and creamy béchamel.

Ingredients

  • 12 pieces lasagna sheets
  • 17.9 ounces ground beef
  • 1.7 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 pieces onion
  • 3 pieces garlic cloves
  • 7.1 ounces mozzarella cheese
  • 3.6 ounces parmesan cheese
  • 2.1 cups milk
  • 1.8 ounces butter
  • 1.8 ounces flour
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare meat sauce: Fry diced 1 pieces onion and 3 pieces garlic cloves in oil. Add 17.9 ounces ground beef and cook until browned. Pour in 1.7 cups tomato sauce, season with 1 pinch salt and pepper and simmer for 20 minutes
  2. Make béchamel: Melt 1.8 ounces butter in a pan, stir in 1.8 ounces flour to form a paste. Gradually add 2.1 cups milk whisking continuously until smooth and thick. Season with 1 pinch salt and pepper.
  3. Layer the lasagna: In a baking dish, layer meat sauce, 12 pieces lasagna sheets, béchamel, and 7.1 ounces mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers ending with béchamel on top. Sprinkle 3.6 ounces parmesan cheese over the top.
  4. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 180°C for 45 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 15 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  5. Rest and serve: Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
